About the Project

Sierra Library provides students with a single, easy-to-use place to find course-aligned readings, study guides, and reference materials. We curate trustworthy content, highlight required course resources, and provide simple tools to help learners prepare for class and assessments.

Our goal is to reduce friction so students spend more time learning and less time searching. Instructors can suggest collections and link required readings for their courses.
